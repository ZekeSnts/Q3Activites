Favorite Avengers movies

Movie Details Review
Avengers: Age of Ultron, this movie was directed by Joss Whedon. The Avengers, consisting of Iron Man, Black Widow, The Hulk, Thor, Captain America and Hawkeye, are against Ultron, who is a robot originally designed by Tony Stark and Bruce Banner. I liked this movie because of the scenes it was able to show. The emotions of each character was shown very well. Even though, Ultron is a robot, he was able to show malice and cruelty as if he was also a human. I would give this movie an 8/10.
Avengers:Infinity War, this movie was directed by Anthony Russo and Joe Russo. The Avengers, now with Black Panther and the Guardians of the Galaxy, are against Thanos, a strong warlord who wants to cut the entire population of the universe in half, believing that it is beneficient to all, and does this by obtaining the infinity stones. This move is one of the best movies I have ever watched solely because the scenes are well directed and the actors did a great job, but this movie also ended in a cliffhanger which made it better. I would rate this movie a 9.5/10.
Avengers:Endgame, was directed by Anthony Russo and Joe Russo. After Thanos accomplished his goal, the Avengers are left with only half of their team. After 5 years, Scott Lang returns from the quantum realm and tells everyone that there is a way to reverse what Thanos did. This movie is one of the best movies I have ever watched because the pacing was good and did not feel rushed, the actors did an amazing job and I got to see all my favorite characters fight against one of the greatest villains.
